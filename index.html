Appliance Energy Use in Australia

This sites gives a simple overview of household appliance energy consumption in the Australian market.

Did you know?

  • Refrigerators and televisions are among the top energy users in households.
  • Star ratings on appliances help compare long-term energy costs.
  • Simple behaviour changes, such as switching devices off at the wall, can save significant energy each year.

Why energy efficiency matters

Energy-efficient appliances not only cut electricity bills but also support Australia’s transition toward cleaner energy. Choosing wisely today creates benefits that last for the lifetime of the product.
